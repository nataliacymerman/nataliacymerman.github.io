my story

I am interdisciplinary creative mind with a knack for Service Design and passion for ethical technology.

I love to learn new things by doing, dreaming, failing, exploring, asking questions and sharing knowledge.

My entire professional life has been dedicated to design; I began as an architect and have since worked as an interior designer and 3D artist. Then I shifted my focus to graphic design, and now I've been working in UX and Service Design for more than three years. Eventually, I believe this is a place where I feel at ease and see potential for growth.

This year, I realized a long-held ambition by becoming a design lecturer and I started being one at Krakow's University of Economics. Contact with students instills in me a sense of wonder and a desire to share what I've learned.

Futuristic is my number one talent in the CliftonStrengths test.
